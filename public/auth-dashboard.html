Follow these steps to enable @buddy in ALL your Figma files:

  1. Go to Figma Developer Portal
  2. Click "Create Webhook" or "New Webhook"
  3. Fill in the details:
    • Name: Figma AI Buddy - Account Level
    • Description: AI design critique assistant for all files
  4. Configure the webhook:
    • Event Type: FILE_COMMENT
    • Scope: All files or Account level
    • Webhook URL: https://buddy-lac-five.vercel.app/api/figma-webhook-account-simple
  5. Click "Save" or "Create Webhook"
  6. Test by adding @buddy analyze this design to any Figma file
